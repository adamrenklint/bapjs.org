Bap was created by {{link "http://adamrenklint.com" "Adam Renklint" target="_blank"}} in Berlin april-may 2015, and was first introduced at the [premiere WebAudio Berlin meetup](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_irRu7FA7Zs).

The latest version is ```v{{site.stableVersion}}``` and was released on {{site.lastReleaseDate}}. The [source code](https://github.com/adamrenklint/bap) and [detailed changelog](https://github.com/adamrenklint/bap/blob/master/CHANGELOG.md) is available on GitHub under the MIT license.

## Feedback and issues

Feedback, questions and bug reports on [Github Issues](https://github.com/adamrenklint/bap/issues) are much appreciated. [Twitter](http://twitter.com/adamrenklint) and [Gitter](https://gitter.im/adamrenklint/bap) also works, or just [send me an email](mailto:adam@renklint.com).

## Roadmap and vision

- roadmap and vision
  - v0.3: effects and chains
  - v0.x: 12-bitcrusher without delay
  - bap-cli: scaffolding, dev server
  - baplets: live coding playground + sharing and forking + embeddable player/editor
